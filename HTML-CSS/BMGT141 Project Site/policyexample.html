Sample Anti-Discrimination Policy

We are an equal opportunity employer. The management team here is committed to ensuring that every employee is treated fairly. Our goal is to provide a safe and productive workplace for all. We have a zero tolerance for discrimination in any form, including but not limited to:

  • Age
  • Marital Status
  • Ancestry
  • Place of Origin
  • Race
  • Creed
  • Ethnic Origin
  • Sex
  • Health or Pregnancy status
  • Family Status
  • Sexual Orientation
  • Disability
Management has a responsability to:
  • Respond without hesitation to any and all violations of the policy
  • Conduct themselves in a professional manner at all times
  • Assign work to employees who best fit the job description and required abilities
  • Maintain a high moral atmosphere in the workplace
Employees have a responsibility to:
  • Avoid any action or inaction that can be perceived as discrimination
  • Help maintain a zero tolerance for harassment and discrimination
  • Report incidents of harassment and discrimination or retaliation as soon as they happen
Incident Resolution Procedure:

An employee must make the offendable action known to the other party or parties involved. If behavior does not stop, an employee must reach out to human resources within a reasonable amount of time.

All complaints will remain anonymous and will be attended to in a timely manner.

Escalated complaints can be filed at your nearest EEOC office.

There will be no biased approach to investigating and resolving any and all matters of discrimination.
