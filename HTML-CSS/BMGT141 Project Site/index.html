Discrimination -

treatment or consideration of, or making a distinction in favor of or against, based on group, class, or category membership rather than individual merit

Just one of many problems found in our constantly diversifying organizations, discrimination - illegal or not - is a tremendous impedance to progress. Adding to the struggle is the fact that those doing the discriminating may not be aware of it at all, while those witnessing it may feel powerless to intervene. This site aims to help employers and workers alike identify discrimination in its various forms, and to provide guidance for stopping and preventing it.
